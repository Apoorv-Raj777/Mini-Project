About Sarthi

A smarter, privacy-first way to understand and navigate urban safety — built from community insights and real-time data.

What Sarthi Does

Sarthi collects quick safety impressions and blends them with contextual map data to build a time-aware understanding of how safe a place feels.

How It Works

Audit reports are enriched with time-decay logic, nearby POIs, transit stops, and history. This powers dynamic heatmaps and safe-route suggestions.

Why It Matters

It helps individuals make confident mobility decisions while giving planners actionable insights — all with strong privacy protections built in.
